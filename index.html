Tips for first car buyers.

1. Set a budget: Determine how much you can afford to spend on your car,
considering not only the purchase price but also ongoing costs like insurance, maintenance, fuel, and taxes.

2. Research: Spend time researching different makes and models that fit within your budget. Consider factors like reliability, fuel efficiency, safety ratings, and maintenance costs.

3. Inspect and test drive: Once you've narrowed down your options, inspect and test drive the cars you're interested in. Pay attention to how the car feels to drive, its comfort,
visibility, and any strange noises or warning lights.
